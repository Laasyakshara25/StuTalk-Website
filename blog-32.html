SPACE: A QUESTION MARK

  • Aditya Damaraju
  • Class of 2023

Space, a place you can never understand or underestimate. It has everything from an asteroid of the size of a cricket ball to a galaxy with trillions of stars and planets. Sometimes I wonder how something that complex was created. And when I start to think about space, I let my thoughts voyage me through something that’s just beyond my regular life.

The fact that we all live on a single planet with life and no other being to be found even some millions of kilometers away is quite surprising. I often ask myself a question “Are we really alone in this Universe?” and my mind takes me to two possible doorways. One, if we are the only superior life forms in this endless cosmos then it must be scary to be the only ones. While the second sounds fictional, I strongly believe that we are not alone.

Many things about the Universe are perplexing. The present world has various concepts that are theoretically possible but have no practical proof. Some of them are a wormhole, parallel universes, size of the universe and many more. I always feel like exploring them until I find out the truth behind them. The most interesting concepts that get me lost into my endless thoughts are Wormholes and Black Holes.

And the day is not so far away where we will happen to meet different galactic life forms.

A Wormhole connects two points in space bending time and in higher dimensions, which are not possible in this world. A Black Hole is a region of spacetime that sucks every particle, even the light itself into it.

Interstellar, a movie that depicts the concepts of WormHoles and Black Holes and explains Space Science in an unknown and unproven way. But, it can be quite fascinating if it can be applied to the practical world. A Wormhole acts like a bridge between two points in space, through which we can travel through time and space. A Black Hole is quite real and we do have evidence for black holes in the real-life world.

Well, there are many unexplored miracles in this Universe and our very own galaxy. But the answers and practical proofs for Wormholes, Black Holes, travelling at the light speed, Dark Energy would blow our minds. We may not get all the answers on the internet but we will find them before the END. And I, being a Space Science enthusiast, am on a journey to unravel the Truths of Space.
