SOCIAL MEDIA: ARE WE USING IT IN THE RIGHT?

  • S. Sravani
  • Class of 2023

Social Media has the power to make you a better person and can also bring you down. We can use the platform to develop our skills by following the people who are already experienced in the same industry and are willing to share their knowledge with us. Social Media is not just for entertainment but it’s a platform where you can learn, teach, promote your products, develop skills, influence others, inspire people around you and motivate them. You should be using it to display your work, make great connections, learn how to do things, find solutions, make opportunities, find the right opportunities, and not be jealous of your friends having a larger presence on social media. Also, spreading hate instead of love, comparing yourself instead of getting inspired, and wasting hours by switching from one app to another for no reason.

From the last decade, the number of social media influencers are growing, and are considered as the most successful people, by success, I mean, being satisfied with the work you do. They are conquering hearts and at the same time, they love what they do, which is very important. To become a good influencer, the right content is very much important. Yes, it’s the content that defines our progress, being consistent is also a key factor, being patient enough for numbers to grow and to acquire financial independence. The best thing is that you can choose your preferred skill and help others to learn it.

Social Media isn’t the problem, but the way we are using it is!

Easy-peasy, isn’t it? Favored skill as in anything which is useful like teaching, cooking, dancing, singing, coding, designing, editing, blogging, vlogging, so on and so forth. If you are not so much into showing up on it, social media has its other side which gives you the best opportunity to learn, get inspired, and to know the world better. Simply by being connected to your beloved influencers or your role models on social media, you are actually giving yourself the best time. There are so many hard-working and enthusiastic people who are putting efforts to spread positivity around, trying to help us gain knowledge, and also to entertain us at once. It’s high time we use the internet wisely to become productive and to learn everything in a better way and probably the easiest way.

Through social media, communication became so effective and easy. These platforms are providing plenty of information to help students. They are on the right page and also, they provide useful information about what to learn and where to learn efficiently. Can you imagine anything better than this? When you are tired and want to watch a good movie but are often confused as to where to watch, whether on Amazon or Netflix or any other site. Well, social media handles have answers to all your questions, see the problem is solved! In the same way, we have many other advantages. Social Media isn’t the problem but the way we are using it is the problem!
