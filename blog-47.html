Being Clueless

  • Nikunj Wadgeri
  • Class of 2023

The literal definition of clueless is completely or hopelessly bewildered, unaware, ignorant, or foolish. And to my surprise, I am being the same these days. YES, completely clueless and hopeless not just about my career but in life and I am desperate to end this bewilderment. This feeling gravitates many to go unhinged.

What exactly does being clueless feel like? It’s a phase in your life where “Everyone I know is either getting married or getting promoted, and I’m getting a cup of coffee! And it's not even for me!” as Rachel said it from F.R.I.E.N.D.S. Let’s dig deep into it, it is actually when you see all the crowd around you progressing and you are unsure of what exactly you want to or need to do. The phase where you are uncertain about almost everything in your life. Yet you are desperate to unravel your life.

"We all end up being clueless at some point in our lives."

Have you ever developed a complex where some of your friends or cousins figured out what they exactly want? Well, that complex is one of the most rotten feelings any human can ever experience. This tends to make you think what’s wrong with you? Why are you so unsorted? Well, these questions may help you to figure out what you need, and end up at square one again. Confused and clueless, about where to begin.

Being Clueless is the last thing anyone wants to be and so do I. Each one of us has been here, to find a riddle of being clueless. But, did you solve this riddle? If the answer is NO, then it is okay and it is also okay not to have a plan, be confused, feel out of place, and much more.

But what you can do to solve this is, take a break from the chaos you have surrounded yourself with. Take a long breath and brace yourself to give a hard one on that cluelessness. By the end of the day, everything will fall in its place.
