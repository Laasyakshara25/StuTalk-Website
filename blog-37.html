WOMEN AND THEIR WORK-LIFE

  • Sameena
  • Class of 2023

The world has heard enough stories about gender equality, women empowerment, and women being equally strong as men, not just physically but mentally. In such a world where women have the freedom to live and work according to their choices, they still face many problems in the real world.

The changes to the gender gap can be brought by industry and women.

As a woman decides to work in the tech industry, she puts efforts beyond her imagination. During her journey, she faces a lot of consequences such as lack of support and understanding. Overcoming these, when she gets selected into one of the MNCs, she tries to speak up but goes unheard because of which she starts losing her self-confidence. Then, there comes harassment. At this point, she has no other option than to leave her job and get married. After her marriage, if she tries to go back to the workplace, she fears upskilling. Despite upskilling herself to match the level of her colleagues, she might fail to balance her personal and professional life.

Some of the solutions are unbiased training and mentorship programs. Let's start from the industry; equal pay helps them to feel motivated and work more at the same time. If there are fewer women in any department, try to hire more women with a good set of skills. There must be sessions to amplify women's voices. Acknowledging their capabilities and skills will boost their self-esteem. A healthy work environment in a company increases the employees' productivity.
