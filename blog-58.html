Why Should You Travel More?

  • Omar Jamaal
  • Class of 2024

Everyone keeps saying how important it is to travel. But, why do people actually love traveling? More importantly, why should we travel more? The benefits of traveling are not just a one-time thing like relaxation for a while. Here are some of the main benefits of traveling and I’m sure that once you get started with traveling, you’ll find some more by yourself;

1. TRAVELING IMPROVES YOUR HEALTH
From cutting down on stress to lowering your chances of developing heart disease, the health benefits of traveling are huge. Traveling more will likely have a tremendous impact on your mental well-being, especially if you're not used to moving out of your comfort zone.
2. TRAVELING HELPS YOU DISCONNECT FROM YOUR DAILY LIFE
This is closely related to my previous point. We often get caught up in our daily lives that sometimes, by simply sticking around, we may do ourselves more harm than good. So, it’s good to disconnect from your everyday chores and take a break once in a while to relax and come back afresh.
3. TRAVELING MAKES YOU SMARTER
Traveling helps you gain knowledge as traveling teaches you different languages, cultures, and the history of different places you visit.
4. TRAVELING IMPROVES YOUR UNDERSTANDING OF DIFFERENT CULTURES
Why we travel may often differ from person to person, but traveling always develops empathy and a deeper understanding of other cultures. Being more understanding and tolerant about a culture different from ours is part of making us better and smarter, but I consider it as a benefit of traveling.

Traveling changes you physically and psychologically in a good way.

5. TRAVELING MAKES YOU TRY AMAZING FOOD
Eating local food from a new place that you are visiting is entirely a pleasant experience. All the diverse flavors teach you more about the place.
6. TRAVELING WIDENS YOUR NETWORK
While traveling, you meet different people in different countries and you might become friends with them. This is the most exciting part of traveling where you get to meet people of diverse cultures, interact with them, and share knowledge.
7. TRAVELING HELPS YOU CREATE A LIFETIME OF MEMORIES
The memories we create while traveling are going to live with us forever. The fun times we have while traveling, the difficulties, the fights, everything makes it a wholesome experience.
8. TRAVELING MAKES YOU LOVE YOUR HOME EVEN MORE
“The magic thing about home is that it feels good to leave, and it feels even better to come back”. You will truly understand the meaning of those words only upon your return home. Your thoughts would be like, “I’m bored after living my whole life in the same place, I need to get out and have a change of scenery”. I was always focusing only on the negative part on how there isn’t much to do around, how you always meet the same people, how nothing changes. But now, when I come home, I’m glad to see all the familiar people and places, how I see only the positive side.
