QUESTION YOURSELF FOR A BETTER CHANGE

  • S. Jeevana Reddy
  • Class of 2023

I have a question for all of you. What is the biggest change or step that you took in your life? How serious are you about your career?

We all have become so lethargic that we are constantly postponing our responsibilities since this pandemic. We are addicted to social media platforms and purely wasting our time. We are putting our precious time in useless things that give us nothing. Do not spend much time on things like social media that are of no use. If you are really interested, do it along with productive things.

Every night, at the end of the day, question yourself, "What new thing have I learned today?" or "How many things have I implemented from what I have learned from yesterday?". Question yourself. What changes do I make to become a more responsible person and be determined towards my work? Any small thing you learn, learn it with curiosity and utmost sincerity. Don’t ever think that you are weaker than others. They may not have the same qualities that you do and it's good. But, don’t discourage yourself by looking at another person Be honest and true to yourself. Move out of your comfort zone to get things done. This is the age where we have plenty of time to learn new things.

I know some people who question themselves, "Why couldn't I do it? After introspection, others seem to do so well. They give their best in all activities and studies. Then, Why can’t I?" But, it’s okay. You don't have to be better than someone else. Give your best in the things which you are truly interested in. Success will follow you.

If you really want to be a successfulperson, then be curious about learning new things.

It is ok if you want to watch a movie, go and watch it now. But, be genuine to yourself and things that you're doing. Be conscious of what is happening around you. Make yourself a busy person. Keep motivating yourself and keep on learning new things. Control your brain, don't let it control you. Don’t overthink.

Education is more than scoring a good grade or landing a certified job. It enriches or makes you a responsible person. If you are failing in the things you're doing, then it means curiosity is missing within you. Change is good as well as bad. It depends on how you transform it. Don’t be stubborn. Question yourself what you truly want to be. If you are not able to answer that question, it's completely fine. But, don't miss the chance you have in your hand. Most importantly, don’t lose them to your laziness. Give it your 100%. Then, success will follow you. If one way is wrong, try another. Don’t relinquish your efforts after a single attempt. If you don't get answers, ask others, or try to find out one way or another. Don’t just suddenly stop and give up in the middle. Work hard.

You have 24 hours in a day. It completely depends on how you utilize them. Everyone in the world has the same 24 hours. If you think otherwise then, definitely I can tell that you have not utilized your time efficiently. If you are delaying any work, it means you are distracted with something else or lack the interest to do it.Divide your time and work accordingly. Be an organized person. If you are learning new things, keep everything aside, and learn them with patience.

Delaying is not healthy. It just reminds you that you're wasting your very valuable time. It means that you're not serious about your work. You have your own reasons why things are not working out. Yet, question yourself, why are you not able to do it? What is stopping you from doing it?
