We all live in a world of mortality unless we face deadly obstacles in the lives we live in. However toxic our lives are, we cannot stop a few evolutions in industries introducing waste, as well as microorganisms too. Well, science happened to be an ability to notice these theories, not too scientific though, but there are some materials that we may not know can harm us with their toxicity. Basically, the toxin is something that can damage an organism, and its capability is toxicity.

Ever wonder what makes dead bodies in the mortuary stay undecayed for several days? Well, that’s because of the chemical formaldehyde and it is also used to store dead bodies of animals in anatomy lab as well as dead organs, anyway storing dead life doesn’t make it toxic, it is because of its effects. 37 grams of it is a lethal dosage. In a healthy human being, it may lead to deadly cancer. Sugar, the state of happiness in our delicious drinks and dishes is of course less toxic than formaldehyde. We live in a world of peaceful regimens and routine, one of them can be our refreshing coffee. As we know, excess of anything is dangerous to our body, 70 cups of coffee as it consists of 12 grams hence, is considered lethal. If consumed in the limit, we can have a few cups. Then, here comes one of the drugs: nicotine, 0.64 grams of nicotine is considered lethal, to consume such dosage one must dedicate a long time to smoking without damaging their lungs, an empty wallet and empty stomach at once. As the effect is supposed to be neurotoxic, one should smoke a few hundreds of cigars at once to observe the effects.

Dimethylmercury, not of world war though. The person who was working on it was exposed to a drop of it on her glove for 15 seconds and of course took all precautions yet, she was already late. It was told that when one of her former students mentioned that one day her husband saw tears rolling from her eyes and the student asked him if she was suffering from her pain, the man replied that it was her brain that stopped registering pain anymore, 0.0065 grams of it is considered as lethal dosage. It stops muscle contractions as a neurotoxin, may stop heartbeat too, not because there exists no antidote but it's enough to kill one, and touching a yellow one may lead to death. The skin of these frogs was used for these purposes. This one is to be considered as an evolution in a microorganism. As we know the oldest life form that exists on earth is supposed to be bacteria.
