Z. A. R. C (ZONE FOR AUGMENTED REALITY CREATIONS)

  • Kaushik Apoori
  • Class of 2023

I am Kaushik from the same college we all study in.

I always believed in quality over quantity and from my schooling, I dreamt of creating my own identity in the market. In achieving success, I worked hard, executed with brilliance, and took the right steps I had to. I always say that, “I will definitely win but not immediately”.

I waited for an opportunity, which I chose, one out of many, and worked towards it with a team from the past year for our startup named Z. A. R. C (Zone for Augmented Reality Creations). This is the journey of an idea, five brains, a year of hard work, commitment, and one goal to succeed. We started this journey hoping for success, failed many times, but tried again and again to succeed and moved with that hope of succeeding at some point.

Exploring my thoughts and scribbling them on paper sometimes makes me feel more innovative rather than sitting quietly. I always prepare to tune myself to render good services to the needy . Now moving onto a path of dedicating an idea and adopting a technology by which we, the team of Z. A. R. C, want to recreate a learning system by which we can enhance the quality of education in the rural and urban area in the same way. Providing a platform for them and giving them a good learning experience with great technology that we didn't get the chance to have at our age is my dream and my team represents that dream together.

Success is destined to the people who work for it.

Everyone has one point to succeed but we need to wait for it. After struggling so much, failing many times, the end result will be rock solid after such drawbacks. I will always be proud of this idea that was built by me with the support of five members, lessons from the past, and guidance from many. One day, in the future, learning must be different from the existing system and we work hard and smart to make the difference.

I trust that life always provides us with opportunities and we need to utilize them. If we don’t take them, the opportunities go to people behind, moving them ahead of us. I decided to pack my bag of ideas, release them to the global market, wait for the best pick and try my best to execute with my team and I will always be proud to say that I am here because of my family, my teachers, my team, and my past. Recreating myself according to the situation and market is what I prefer the most. Situations keep pushing me back but I try to tune them according to what I prefer and like to do the most because there will be a small war to place an inverted comma in converting the impossible situation to possible which I learned from my English faculty in my childhood. I love to be at war where I can compete with myself all the time. Self-Practicing the thoughts before executing, analyzing the future outcomes of problems, and trying to sort them are few of the best skills in my bag, and the ones I like the most. The skills make me smart, the thoughts make me shine, efforts make me reach closer to success and support makes me stronger.
