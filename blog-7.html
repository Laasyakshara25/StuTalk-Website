THE CHOICES I DON'T REGRET

  • Veda Thangeda
  • Class of 2023

There are a few qualities of mine that make me happy and making my own choices definitely tops the list. Be it choosing a career, which impacts the rest of your life, or the flavor of Ice cream that you choose on a particular day. But, it gives me pride in the fact that I never regret the choices I make and stand by them.

Life is all about making choices and living with the choices made. It’s the way of moving ahead in life. We make boundless choices every single day. Some help us excel in life and while others might not. But, standing by your own choice without any regret and constantly working hard to make things better will boost your confidence. Your choices may let you reach new heights or pull you down.

When the latter happens regretting it can do you no good. Instead, standing up to make things better and working on them will give you a good learning experience. Because “BAD CHOICES”, as we call them, are not mistakes. But, great learning experiences. I believe that making the wrong decisions can help you do a lot better in many aspects in the long run. Everyone wants to win, but only a few end up winning. But, when you lose, you thrive to work harder the next time.

MAKING A CHOICE OF YOUR OWN CARVES YOUR WAY TOWARDS BUILDING YOUR OWN LIFE.

We live in an opinion driven society, everyone has a hundred ways to judge and hopefully prove you wrong. Every choice comes with its own challenges. We need to face both bitter and sweet outcomes. Everyone wants to prove themselves correct especially with matters that affect their own life. But, standing by your choice to make things better no matter what will definitely bring out the best version of yourself.

No one is perfect and we don’t have to be. We don’t have to be right. We just have to work hard until we prove that our choices make the best possible impact. Sometimes, we make the right choice. But, when something goes wrong we need to be ready to face the consequences and stand up for ourselves because if we don’t, then no one will.

What are we trying to prove all our lives? For what reason are we working so hard? Well, for me it’s simple: I just want to live a happy life, built on my own terms, and “The Choices I don’t Regret” will make it happen.
