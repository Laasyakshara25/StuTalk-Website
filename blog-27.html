HAPPINESS ALARM

  • Nari Akshitha
  • Class of 2023

Happiness is like a broad smile of unfeigned delight. As we began growing up, we slowly replaced our happiness with anxiety, fear, and other unwanted external feelings. Many of us cannot even determine if we are truly feeling happy without any second-guessing and underestimating even though there are bazillion reasons for a person to be happy. I will not preach obvious things like ‘have strength’. Instead, I will tell you my story!

My happiness is a mirage, a mirage that I have created.

On days when I hate myself for being me, on days when I just want to disappear forever and never return, to cope up with such bad days, I made a room in my heart for all my fond memories. Whenever I'm down, I just ring an alarm which I call the ‘HAPPINESS ALARM’.

It makes me reminisce all my precious memories like a warm smile appearing on the face of my mother when I say "I love you, Mom", it just pulls on my heartstrings. Yesterday, today, tomorrow, and forever, my heart will always be full of desire to find contentment. I believe in the strength of optimism. I’m happy that even in my hardships, I'm me. I like to hear the melody of my alarm that gives me the courage to get up every day and bloom like a rose or flutter like the leaves of a cherry blossom tree.

It would be great if each one of you can preserve your memories like me and ring your own happiness alarm and thrive through the dark times. At last, all I believe or say is everyone has their unique way of being happy. A smile is very contagious, never stop spreading happiness, you never know, maybe your gesture can even ring others' happiness alarm and make their day bright. We are in a world that resonates, so never stop finding happiness in every moment.
How many times are you going to ring the happiness alarm?
