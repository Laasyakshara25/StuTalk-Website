EMBRACING LIFE

  • B Sai Sri Vindhya
  • Class of 2023

Life, as we all know, begins with birth and ceases with death, an inevitable occurrence for everyone. But is there anything more to life than just completing the cycle of life and death? The year 2020 has brought about many changes in both personal and professional life for all.

There have been several highs and numerous lows. But what even is life without these barriers and hurdles? Ultimately, those who have the support of their loved ones and the guts to face their fears are the ones who excel.

There are no shortcuts in life that enable us to skip the testing times and bask in the happier ones. But we can always make a way out of these tough times by looking at the silver lining.

WE MUST GROW THROUGH WHAT WE GO THROUGH.

Life is increasingly becoming a rat race with achieving success being the only objective. People are becoming so self-obsessed that they are not concerned about the people around them. Standing for oneself is never wrong. But, lending a helping hand to the ones in need isn’t a bad choice either.

People around us may be looking out for a shoulder to lean on and we aren’t an exception. Allowing someone into our comfort zone and trusting someone can take a while. But once there is someone to share and let our heart out to, life will become much easier to deal with and a beautiful journey henceforth.

Life is too short to fill it with negativity and hatred. We should try to rise from our failures and evolve to become a better person. Being kind can change our perspective towards life. The world would be a much happier place with a little empathy towards each other.
