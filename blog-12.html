OPPORTUNITIES

  • K. Koushik
  • Class of 2023

Opportunities won't knock on your door, you have to seek them. This may seem basic, but the problem is, people have given up on looking out for opportunities. To prove this, there is a saying, “You wouldn’t see an opportunity even if it hits you in the face”, which most of us can certainly relate to. Opportunity can literally be right where you are, but you turn a blind eye to it.

KNOWLEDGE AND OPPORTUNITIES

Many of us believe "knowledge is power", which is definitely true. You won’t find opportunities without knowledge. Everything you want is just a piece of knowledge away from obtaining. And the only thing between you and massive wealth is the right knowledge.I’ve experienced many delays and pitfalls, but I was able to push beyond them to move on, and I can teach you to do the same because of the knowledge I've obtained.

YOU HAVE TO GO FOR IT

To get hold of opportunities, you have to move out of your comfort zone. Staying with your parents or depending on them, past the age of 25 isn’t going to work. Sorry, but it’s the truth. An opportunity might mean moving out of your own house, living in another country, or working with people you don’t wish to. To grab better chances in life, the above-mentioned steps may be important. Maybe, just maybe, all you need can be a passport as a ticket for the best opportunity in your life.

The opportunity may not be where you are, because “You might be the big fish in a small pond’.

CONTACTS: ROOT FOR OPPORTUNITIES

To achieve anything you want, “contacts equal contracts” may be the key. How big is your power base? How big is your circle? The more people you know, the more opportunities will come your way or at least show you a path, probably the right one. There’s a reason why most of the successful people have a huge network. Hope you realized the reason at the start of the above paragraph. Your next opportunity might just be with someone you don’t even know yet. You need to go out, meet people, and accept them.

IN THE MIDDLE OF DIFFICULTIES LIES OPPORTUNITIES

Anything and everything you want to achieve by grabbing a single opportunity, many may have already achieved it and it’s true. But, this can never be your point of demotivation. Because one can never rule out the fact that every person must have had a different opportunity to prove the same thing. You are no different. You can never know where your opportunity lies or comes from. Be ready to welcome every opportunity to prove yourself as a BETTER HUMAN BEING.
