My Brother

  • Prajun Panduga
  • Class of 2023

My brother’s name is Pavan Kumar. He is my elder brother and the one who is always positive towards his work, a well mannered and a disciplined person. He is now the pillar of our house. He always thinks about us. He is a good student at what he studied. He completed his MBA this year and now he is focusing on his job. My brother is good at everything and my connection with my brother is something I will be unable to express and always fall short of words to describe him. He is my mentor, best friend, guardian, and caretaker. Honestly, he is my everything. Without him, I'm nothing. Today, I’m here because of my brother, but at the same time, he is the world’s most annoying person.

He will always tease me. We fight, scold each other and within a few minutes, we become normal as if the fight never happened. My mother wouldn’t interfere because she knows that our brothers’ rivalry will not continue for a long period. In our childhood, we usually fought a lot but we never threw words. We both did work that our parents assigned. My brother would always understand my problem and figure out the solution for it.

"If I reach my goals one day, it’s because of him."

Sometimes he indirectly gives me solutions when I'm confused. Because he says that if I always help you, you won’t use your brain in my absence so use your brain to solve your problems. He always says that this is your work, you have to complete it individually, no one will help you. Until and unless you use your brain 100 percent, you won’t complete it. He scolds me if I make a mistake. After that, he will give a lecture to me and he usually warns me to take the right path, as at this age everything will be attractive so don’t be attracted to that.

He says all that glitters is not gold, all the blue stones are not diamonds, so think wisely when you want to act. He is not just my elder brother, he is my father figure. He does everything for me that a father would do for his children. He sacrificed his final stage of student life for me due to financial problems. When we both were staying at the hostel, my parents were unable to pay the hostel fee to us. So he would go home after staying 20 days in the hostel. He would go to college only to take his exams. He knows that after this I cannot attend college. But he never asked me to stay at home or wanted to enjoy his student life. He thought about me and my future. If I wanted to do anything, I would inform my brother first, not my parents because he knows me better than anyone.

He is two and a half years older than me. Even if I wash his feet and sprinkle that water on my head it will not be enough for his duty towards me. Remember that no one can separate us. I wish even if death asks us who would want to die first, I would die to let my brother live and that is how much I admire my brother. I have a lot of gratitude for him. I wish him to stay happy, achieve his goals and make us more proud than ever.
