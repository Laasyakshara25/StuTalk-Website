Do We Have A Healthy Life?

  • Rachana
  • Class of 2024

Is the meaning of a healthy life always concerned with our physical health? Definitely not. We are frequently questioned about our physical health but not our mental health, which is just as important as our physical health. So how do we make one??

The first step to a peaceful life is to prioritise ourselves. We also need to concentrate on our physical health to be better in both ways. We can do it by eating well, getting adequate sleep, and eating vitamins and nutrient-rich food. Lack of sleep can make you cranky.

Then, the important thing which we don’t talk about much is our mental health. Honestly, it’s not easy to be stress-free all the time, but what can we do to improve healthy habits? It is important to take care of ourselves by investing time in self-care, identifying our strengths and weaknesses, or pursuing a new hobby that can help. Poorly managed emotions could be harmful and it's best to talk to someone we trust when we're frustrated, sad, or angry.

There is hope, even when your brain tells you there isn't ~John Green

Focus on the current situation and not let the past bring you down because we can only handle our present to make a better future. Being lonely for a long time could be dangerous too. So try socialising and talking things over, which is the solution to many problems. We might experience anxiety at times that are terrifying and might not always be under control, and those are the times you must not be alone and work on stuff you truly enjoy.

Also, staying physically active does not always mean spending hours at the gym but staying active through any physical activity that we would like to do. Challenging yourself could be one activity where you can learn new things, something that can boost your confidence.

Don’t worry about what you shouldn’t be eating. Instead, eat what you like now and then. Feel free to say no to things you don’t want to do and set boundaries that could relieve your stress. Remember that dreams have no limits. Do not underestimate yourself and constantly work on it. Everyone is not born to pursue the same activity. These are a few ways to maintain a healthy life.
